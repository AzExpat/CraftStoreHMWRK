Ragtime Beaded Bracelet

This is what you will be making

Color Study Part One:

In Part One, select various beads in one predominant color category. In this example, the sample used all Picasso Sea Foam Turquoise Green in Tilas, Chexx, 8/0’s and 6/0’s. The Tuff cord and leather are also in the same color family. So, whether you make a gray bracelet or a copper one, think of this first part as a statement of color in various shapes. You will need for Part One:

  • 2-1/2 yards Leather
  • 2 yards Tuff Cord
  • Chexx Beads, Tila Beads
  • 6/0 Seed Beads, 8/0 Seed Beads
  • Button, Flush Cutters
  • B’s Wax, Thread Zap II and GS Hypo Cement

String your button to the mid-way point of your leather and strap your project to the Design Tray. It will look similar to this photo to the left. If you need a refresher on how to do this, refer to our Skill Builder: How to Secure Your Project to a Board.
